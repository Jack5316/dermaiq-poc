# DermaIQ: Decoding Skincare Ingredients with AI

## The Problem

Every day, consumers apply dozens of skincare ingredients to their bodies without understanding what they're putting on their skin. The average skincare product contains 15-50 ingredients, often listed with complex scientific names that are unintelligible to the average person. This lack of transparency creates significant challenges:

- **87%** of consumers struggle to understand ingredient labels
- **92%** are concerned about potentially harmful chemicals
- **76%** want better information about their skincare products
- **68%** would pay for personalized ingredient analysis

The skincare industry is projected to reach $189.3 billion globally by 2025, yet consumers remain largely in the dark about what they're purchasing and applying to their bodies.

## Our Solution

DermaIQ is an AI-powered iOS application that instantly decodes skincare ingredient labels, providing consumers with reliable, transparent information about the products they use every day.

### Key Features

- **Instant Label Scanning**: Point your camera at any skincare product to instantly analyze its ingredients
- **Plain-Language Explanations**: Complex scientific terms translated into easy-to-understand descriptions
- **Safety Assessments**: Identification of potentially harmful ingredients with scientific explanations
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Tailored product suggestions based on your skin type and concerns
- **Product Comparisons**: Side-by-side ingredient analysis of multiple products

### How It Works

1. **Scan**: Use your iPhone camera to scan a product's ingredient list
2. **Analyze**: Our AI instantly identifies and categorizes each ingredient
3. **Learn**: Receive clear explanations about each ingredient's purpose and potential effects
4. **Decide**: Make informed purchasing decisions based on reliable information
