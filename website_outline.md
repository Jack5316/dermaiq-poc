## User Requirements

### Target Users
- Health-conscious consumers aged 25-45
- Individuals with sensitive skin or skin conditions
- Skincare enthusiasts and beauty influencers
- Dermatologists and skincare professionals

### User Needs
- Quick and accurate ingredient analysis
- Easy-to-understand explanations of complex terms
- Reliable information about ingredient safety
- Personalized recommendations based on skin type
- Ability to compare multiple products

### User Journey
1. **Awareness**: Discover DermaIQ through social media, App Store, or word of mouth
2. **Consideration**: Visit website, read about features and benefits
3. **Decision**: Download app or join waitlist
4. **Onboarding**: Create profile, input skin type and concerns
5. **Usage**: Scan products, learn about ingredients, make informed decisions
6. **Upgrade**: Convert to premium subscription for advanced features
7. **Advocacy**: Share with friends, leave reviews
