# DermaIQ Video Ingenuity Plan

## Overview

This document outlines our strategic plan for leveraging the DermaIQ video presentation beyond the coursework submission. Following Peter Thiel's Zero to One principles, we'll use this video as a powerful tool to establish DermaIQ as a category creator in personalized skincare analysis, build our monopoly position, and create network effects through strategic distribution.

* **Implementation:** Embed the video prominently on the DermaIQ homepage as the primary above-the-fold content
* **Specific Uses:**
  * Auto-play (muted) version with captions as website hero section
  * Full version accessible via prominent "Watch Our Story" button

* **Expected Outcomes:**
  * 30% increase in time spent on site
