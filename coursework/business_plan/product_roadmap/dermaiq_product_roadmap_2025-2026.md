# DermaIQ Product Development Roadmap 2025-2026

## Executive Summary

This document outlines DermaIQ's strategic product development roadmap for the next 18 months, from May 2025 through October 2026. The roadmap is designed to systematically build, enhance, and expand our AI-powered skincare ingredient analysis platform while capitalizing on identified market trends and addressing the needs of our target user personas.

The roadmap is structured in three distinct phases:

1. **Foundation Phase** (May-October 2025): Establish core functionality and minimum viable product
2. **Enhancement Phase** (November 2025-April 2026): Expand features and deepen personalization
3. **Expansion Phase** (May-October 2026): Develop B2B capabilities and new platform integrations

Each phase includes specific features, technical requirements, resource needs, success metrics, and alignment with our key differentiators. This roadmap will guide our development priorities and provide a clear timeline for implementation, helping us achieve our vision of becoming the leading AI-powered skincare ingredient analysis platform.

## Phase 1: Foundation (May-October 2025)

### Overview
The Foundation Phase focuses on establishing the core functionality of DermaIQ, delivering a minimum viable product that addresses the primary pain points identified in our market research. This phase will build the essential infrastructure and user experience that will serve as the foundation for future enhancements.

### Key Milestones
- **MVP Launch**: July 2025
- **Public Beta**: September 2025
- **V1.0 Release**: October 2025

### Feature Development

- **Description**: Develop the fundamental AI system for analyzing skincare ingredients
- **Key Components**:
  - Ingredient database with 5,000+ common skincare ingredients
  - Safety classification algorithm
  - Function identification system
  - Basic ingredient interaction detection
- **Technical Requirements**:
  - Machine learning model for ingredient classification
  - Natural language processing for ingredient name recognition
  - Cloud infrastructure for database hosting
  - API architecture for scalability
- **Resources**:
  - 2 ML engineers, 1 data scientist, 1 backend developer
  - Computing resources for model training
