## 2. Problem Statement

### The Skincare Ingredient Dilemma

![Problem Illustration](https://via.placeholder.com/500x300?text=Problem+Illustration)

- **87%** of consumers struggle to understand skincare ingredient labels
- **92%** are concerned about potentially harmful ingredients
- **64%** have experienced negative reactions to skincare products
- **89%** find current research methods time-consuming

> "I've spent hours researching ingredients only to still feel confused about what I'm putting on my skin."

---

## 3. Solution Overview

### Introducing DermaIQ

![Solution Illustration](https://via.placeholder.com/500x300?text=Solution+Illustration)

**DermaIQ is an AI-powered mobile application that:**

- **Scans** product ingredient labels using computer vision
- **Analyzes** ingredients for safety and compatibility
- **Personalizes** recommendations based on individual skin profiles
- **Simplifies** complex scientific information into actionable insights

> Our mission: Make skincare ingredient information accessible, personalized, and actionable for everyone.
