# DermaIQ: One-Page Business Plan

## Vision
To create a world where consumers understand exactly what they put on their skin and can make truly informed decisions about their skincare products.

## Mission
To empower consumers with transparent, reliable information about skincare ingredients through accessible AI technology.

## Problem Statement
Consumers face significant challenges when trying to understand skincare product ingredients:
- Complex scientific terminology on labels
- Lack of transparency about potential risks
- Time-consuming research process
- Information overload from conflicting sources
- Generic information not tailored to individual needs

## Solution
DermaIQ is an AI-powered iOS application that:
- Scans product labels using computer vision
- Decodes complex scientific terms into plain language
- Identifies potentially harmful ingredients
- Provides personalized recommendations
- Offers product comparisons and alternatives

## Target Market
- **Primary**: Health-conscious consumers (25-45) concerned about skincare ingredients
- **Secondary**: Individuals with sensitive skin or skin conditions
- **Tertiary**: Skincare enthusiasts and beauty influencers
- **Potential B2B**: Dermatologists, estheticians, and skincare brands

## Market Size
- Global skincare market: $189.3B by 2025
- Clean beauty market: $54B by 2025 (growing at 12% annually)
- 87% of consumers struggle to understand ingredient labels
- 76% would use an app that helps decode ingredient lists
- 68% would pay for premium features

## Business Model
**Freemium Model**:
- **Free Tier**: Basic ingredient scanning and information (limited scans per month)
- **Premium Subscription** (£3.99/month): Unlimited scans, personalized recommendations, product comparisons

**Additional Revenue Streams**:
- B2B API access for skincare brands and professionals
- Anonymized market research data for skincare companies
- Affiliate marketing commission from recommended products

## Competitive Advantage
- Advanced AI technology for more accurate ingredient recognition
- Comprehensive database of 20,000+ skincare ingredients
- Scientific backing through partnerships with dermatologists
- Personalized recommendations based on individual skin profiles
- User-friendly interface making complex information accessible

## Marketing Strategy
- **Content Marketing**: Educational blog posts and social media content about skincare ingredients
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborations with skincare influencers and dermatologists
- **App Store Optimization**: Strategic keywords and compelling visuals
- **PR Outreach**: Features in beauty and tech publications
- **Community Building**: User forums and social media groups
