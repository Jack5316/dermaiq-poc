## Slide 2: Vision & Mission

**Vision:**  
A world where consumers understand exactly what they put on their skin and can make truly informed decisions about their skincare products.

**Mission:**  
To empower consumers with transparent, reliable information about skincare ingredients through accessible AI technology.

## Slide 3: The Problem

- **Complex Terminology**: Ingredient labels use scientific names that are difficult for average consumers to understand
- **Lack of Transparency**: Limited information about potential risks or benefits of specific ingredients
- **Information Overload**: Too much conflicting information online about skincare ingredients
- **Time-Consuming Research**: Researching each ingredient manually is impractical for most consumers
- **Personalization Gap**: Generic information doesn't account for individual skin types or conditions

## Slide 4: Our Solution

**DermaIQ: AI-Powered Skincare Ingredient Analysis**

- **Scan** product labels using computer vision to identify ingredients
- **Decode** complex scientific terms into plain language
- **Alert** users to potentially harmful ingredients based on scientific research
- **Recommend** products based on individual skin profiles and preferences
- **Educate** users about skincare ingredients through an intuitive interface

## Slide 5: How It Works

1. **Scan**: Point your iPhone camera at any skincare product ingredient list
2. **Analyze**: Our AI instantly identifies and categorizes each ingredient
3. **Learn**: Receive clear explanations about each ingredient's purpose and potential effects
4. **Compare**: View alternative products with safer or more effective ingredients
5. **Decide**: Make informed purchasing decisions based on reliable information
